About Us

We take pride in introducing ourselves as one of the country’s leading manufacturer of corrugated boxes. In fact we are specialized in providing total packaging solutions since 1976.

Carton Kraft Manufacturers is one of the Pakistan’s largest manufacturers and suppliers of plain and printed corrugated packing boxes and all types of industrial and commercial corrugated packaging cartons using latest automatic machines. We procure raw material from certified vendors all around the world to manufacture our extensive range of products. These are light in weight and possess excellent weight bearing capacity and strength, catering to the varied requirements of various industries such as pharmaceuticals, consumer, home appliances, food products, engineering equipment, glass and other fragile items.

Our Products

Master Cartons

Master cartons are durable and standardized packaging containers used to transport and safeguard goods during shipping. They provide an outer layer of protection and facilitate easy handling, stacking, and storage. Master cartons are crucial for e-commerce and retail operations, ensuring that products remain secure and intact throughout the supply chain, ultimately reaching their destination in optimal condition.

Inner Cartons

Inner cartons are compact and robust packaging units that provide an extra layer of protection and organization for individual products. They ensure the safety and integrity of items during transportation and storage. With their space-saving design and durability, inner cartons optimize packaging efficiency, prevent damage, and facilitate streamlined inventory management. Whether in e-commerce or retail settings, inner cartons are essential for maintaining packaging quality and optimizing supply chain processes.

Designed Cartons

Designed cartons are customized packaging solutions that not only protect products but also serve as effective marketing tools. They feature visually appealing graphics, logos, and colors that enhance brand recognition and engage consumers. These cartons create a memorable unboxing experience and communicate the quality and value of the enclosed items. With their attractive design and functional structure, designed cartons elevate product presentation and leave a lasting impression on customers, contributing to overall brand perception and customer satisfaction.

Printed Cartons

Printed cartons are custom packaging solutions that blend branding and product information. They feature visually appealing designs, logos, and essential details to attract customers and communicate brand messaging effectively. With their high-quality printing techniques, printed cartons create a memorable unboxing experience while reinforcing brand identity and increasing product recognition. These cartons play a vital role in captivating consumers and elevating the overall packaging presentation.

Shipping Cartons

Shipping cartons are durable containers crafted for safe and efficient product transportation. Designed to withstand handling, impact, and environmental factors, these sturdy cartons protect items during transit. They adhere to industry standards and optimize space for seamless logistics operations. Shipping cartons are essential for safeguarding products, ensuring their arrival in excellent condition, and contributing to smooth supply chain processes.

Die Cut Boxes

Die-cut boxes are custom-shaped packaging solutions that offer a tailored and secure fit for products. With precise cutting techniques, these boxes eliminate excess space and provide enhanced protection during transit. They come in various shapes and sizes, catering to specific product needs and branding requirements. Die-cut boxes are versatile and suitable for retail packaging, e-commerce shipments, and promotional displays. With their precise craftsmanship and customized design options, die-cut boxes elevate product presentation and strengthen brand recognition.

Food Boxes

Food boxes are purpose-built packaging solutions for the food industry, prioritizing safety and freshness. These boxes use food-grade materials, offer grease resistance, and control temperature for optimal food preservation during storage and transportation. Available in diverse sizes and designs, they accommodate various food items like pizzas, sandwiches, and baked goods. Food boxes are indispensable for food delivery services, takeout orders, and catering, ensuring convenient and hygienic packaging that maintains food integrity and enhances the dining experience.

Fruit Boxes

Fruit boxes are purpose-built containers for storing and transporting fresh fruits. These breathable boxes preserve the quality and freshness of the fruits while protecting them from damage. Available in different sizes, fruit boxes accommodate a variety of fruit types. They play a vital role in the agricultural and retail sectors, ensuring that fruits maintain their taste, texture, and nutritional value until they reach consumers. Fruit boxes provide a reliable packaging solution that upholds fruit quality and enhances the overall supply chain process.

Stiffners

Stiffeners play a crucial role in packaging companies by providing structural support and stability to packaging materials. These inserts or reinforcements are strategically placed to prevent bending, folding, or damage during transportation. Made from materials like cardboard, foam, or plastic, stiffeners enhance package durability and ensure product protection. They are vital for maintaining the integrity of goods and minimizing the risk of damage, resulting in improved customer satisfaction and reduced product loss for packaging companies.

Two Pieces Setup Boxes

Two-piece setup boxes are elegant and versatile packaging solutions that provide a premium look for luxury products. With a separate lid and base, these boxes are commonly used for items like jewelry and cosmetics. They offer durability and protection while allowing for customization in size, finish, and printing. Two-piece setup boxes elevate product aesthetics and create a memorable unboxing experience, making them ideal for businesses focused on luxury presentation.

Folded Boxes

Folded boxes are versatile and practical packaging solutions that provide reliable protection and convenience. They are easily assembled from flat materials and come in various sizes and styles to meet different packaging needs. These boxes offer cost-effective and space-saving solutions for secure product storage and transportation.

Handeled Boxes

Handled boxes are user-friendly packaging solutions with built-in handles for easy carrying. They ensure secure handling, come in various sizes and materials, and are suitable for retail, e-commerce, and promotional purposes.
